## Document Suite

### 1. Privacy Policy (`01-Privacy-Policy.md`)
**Purpose:** Defines how QuickBooks Time-Warp handles user data and complies with privacy regulations.

**Key Features:**
- Emphasizes 100% local processing (no cloud transmission)
- GDPR and CCPA compliance language
- Clear data retention policies
- No collection of personal information
- Contact information for privacy concerns
- Transparent data handling procedures

**Use Case:** Required for legal compliance, customer transparency, and regulatory adherence. Should be made available to all users and referenced during installation.

---

### 2. Ethics Statement (`02-Ethics-Statement.md`)
**Purpose:** Articulates the company's commitment to ethical business practices and professional integrity.

**Key Features:**
- Commitment to data confidentiality
- Client-first principles
- Professional integrity standards
- Transparency in operations
- Responsible stewardship of financial data
- Compliance with accounting standards
- Whistleblower protection
- Continuous improvement culture

**Use Case:** Demonstrates corporate values, builds trust with customers, and sets expectations for employee conduct. Useful for marketing materials and customer due diligence processes.

---

### 3. Terms of Service / EULA (`03-Terms-of-Service-EULA.md`)
**Purpose:** Defines the legal agreement between software provider and users.

**Key Features:**
- License grant and restrictions
- Acceptable use policy
- Mandatory backup requirements
- User responsibilities
- Warranty disclaimers
- Limitation of liability
- Prohibited uses (fraud, tax evasion, etc.)
- Termination conditions
- Dispute resolution procedures

**Use Case:** Must be accepted by users before software installation. Protects both parties legally and sets clear expectations for software use.

---

### 4. Data Security & Handling Policy (`04-Data-Security-Handling-Policy.md`)
**Purpose:** Comprehensive security framework for protecting sensitive financial data.

**Key Features:**
- Local processing architecture (zero-transmission)
- AES-256 encryption standards
- Secure file handling procedures
- Automatic working file cleanup
- Access controls and authentication
- Audit trail capabilities
- Incident response procedures
- Security best practices for users
- Compliance with security frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001)

**Use Case:** Essential for security audits, compliance certifications, and customer security assessments. Demonstrates commitment to data protection.

---

### 5. Support & Service Agreement (`05-Support-Service-Agreement.md`)
**Purpose:** Defines technical support services and service level commitments.

**Key Features:**
- Support tier definitions (Standard, Premium, Emergency)
- Response time commitments and SLAs
- Professional service standards
- Escalation procedures
- Remote support policies
- Professional services offerings (consulting, training)
- Knowledge base and self-service resources
- Customer responsibilities

**Use Case:** Sets clear expectations for support services, defines service levels, and provides framework for customer satisfaction.

## Customization Notes

### Items to Update Before Use

**Throughout all documents, replace placeholders:**

1. **[JURISDICTION]** - Insert appropriate jurisdiction for governing law (e.g., "the State of Delaware")

2. **Contact Information** - Verify and update:
   - Phone numbers for support
   - Physical addresses if needed
   - Emergency contact numbers
   - Department-specific email addresses

3. **Company-Specific Information:**
   - Legal entity names (verify exact corporate names)
   - State of incorporation
   - Business registration numbers (if required in your jurisdiction)

4. **License Terms:**
   - Specific pricing tiers
   - License types offered
   - Subscription vs. perpetual terms
   - Refund policies (review with finance/legal)

5. **Support Hours:**
   - Confirm actual support availability
   - Update timezone references if needed
   - Verify holiday schedule

6. **Technical Specifications:**
   - Confirm supported OS versions
   - Verify QuickBooks version compatibility
   - Update hardware requirements

### Legal Review Required

**IMPORTANT:** Before publishing or using these documents:

1. **Engage Legal Counsel:**
   - Have an attorney licensed in your jurisdiction review all documents
   - Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal laws
   - Verify warranty disclaimers and liability limitations are enforceable
   - Confirm dispute resolution procedures are appropriate

2. **Industry-Specific Requirements:**
   - Consult with accountant regarding financial software regulations
   - Verify compliance with industry standards (AICPA, etc.)
   - Review requirements for handling financial data in your market

3. **Insurance Review:**
   - Provide to professional liability insurance carrier
   - Verify alignment with insurance policy terms
   - Ensure adequate coverage for stated liabilities

4. **Jurisdiction-Specific Considerations:**
   - Some jurisdictions limit liability disclaimers
   - Consumer protection laws may impose additional requirements
   - International operations may require translations and local adaptations

## Version Control and Updates

### Maintaining Current Documents

**Regular Review Schedule:**
- Annual comprehensive review (recommended: Q1 each year)
- Quarterly quick review for minor updates
- Immediate review when regulations change
- Post-incident review if security issues arise

**Update Tracking:**
- Increment version number with each change
- Update "Last Updated" date
- Maintain changelog of modifications
- Archive previous versions

**Change Communication:**
- Notify users of material changes via email
- Display update notification in software
- Post announcement on website
- Provide 30-day notice when possible

### Version Numbering

- **Major changes (1.0 → 2.0):** Significant policy changes, new legal requirements
- **Minor changes (1.0 → 1.1):** Clarifications, contact updates, minor additions
- **Patches (1.1 → 1.1.1):** Typo corrections, formatting improvements

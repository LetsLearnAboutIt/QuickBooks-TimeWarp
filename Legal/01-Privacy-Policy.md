## 🔒 YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES YOUR COMPUTER

QuickBooks Time-Warp® is the **ONLY** solution of its kind that allows you to migrate your QuickBooks data **WITHOUT** sending your company file to a vendor.

✅ Everything runs locally on **YOUR** computer  
✅ **NO** file uploads to cloud services  
✅ **NO** remote access to your data  
✅ **NO** data retention — we don't collect, store, or transmit **ANY** information  

Your financial data remains completely private and under your control.

Unlike other QuickBooks conversion services that require you to upload your company file to their servers, grant remote access, or trust a third party with your financial data — QuickBooks Time-Warp® operates **entirely** on your local computer. No internet connection is required for migration. No data ever leaves your machine.

## 1. Introduction

Our System Administrator (oursystemadmin.com) and Live Remote Support, Inc (liveremotesupport.net) (collectively, "we," "us," or "our") are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how QuickBooks Time-Warp software ("Software") processes data and outlines our privacy practices.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of QuickBooks Time-Warp software, which is designed to facilitate data migration and date adjustment operations on QuickBooks company files.

## 3. Data We Process

### 3.1 QuickBooks Financial Data
The Software processes the following types of data from your QuickBooks company files:
- Transaction records (invoices, bills, payments, etc.)
- Customer and vendor information
- Inventory data
- Chart of accounts
- Date and timestamp information
- Company configuration settings
- Historical financial records

### 3.2 Technical Operation Data
During normal operation, the Software may temporarily create:
- Working files for data processing
- Backup copies (if enabled by user)
- Log files for troubleshooting purposes
- Temporary migration files

## 4. How We Process Data

### 4.1 Local Processing Only
**IMPORTANT:** QuickBooks Time-Warp operates entirely on your local computer system. We do NOT:
- Transmit your data to cloud servers
- Upload your data to the internet
- Send your financial information to our servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Access your data remotely without explicit permission

### 4.2 Processing Purposes
Data is processed solely for the following purposes:
- Date adjustment and migration operations
- Data validation and integrity checking
- Creation of migrated QuickBooks files
- Generation of migration reports (stored locally)

### 4.3 Temporary Files
The Software creates temporary working files during the migration process. These files:
- Are stored in your local system's temporary directory
- Contain copies of transaction data being processed
- Are automatically deleted upon successful completion
- Can be manually removed using the cleanup function

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 No Long-Term Storage
We do not retain your QuickBooks data. All processing occurs in real-time during migration operations.

### 5.2 Working Files
- **During Migration:** Temporary working files exist only during active processing
- **After Completion:** All temporary files are automatically deleted
- **User Control:** You maintain full control over source and destination files

### 5.3 Log Files
Technical log files (if enabled) may contain:
- File names processed
- Operation timestamps
- Error messages (no financial data)
- Performance metrics

These logs are stored locally and can be deleted at any time.

## 6. Personal Information

### 6.1 No Collection of Personal Information
QuickBooks Time-Warp does NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- User names or contact information
- Email addresses
- Payment information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Behavioral data

### 6.2 License Information
Software licensing and activation are managed through separate systems operated by Our System Administrator and Live Remote Support, Inc. License validation may require internet connectivity but does not transmit your QuickBooks data.

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security practices:
- Local encryption during processing operations
- Secure file handling protocols
- Memory sanitization after operations
- Protection against unauthorized access

### 7.2 User Responsibilities
You are responsible for:
- Maintaining security of your local systems
- Creating backups before using the Software
- Protecting access to QuickBooks files
- Securing your work environment

## 8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland:

### 8.1 Data Controller
You (the user) are the data controller of your QuickBooks financial data. Our Software acts as a data processor operating under your instruction.

### 8.2 Legal Basis
Processing is based on:
- **Contractual Necessity:** To perform the migration services you've requested
- **Legitimate Interest:** To ensure data integrity and successful migration

### 8.3 Your Rights
As a data controller, you retain all rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

Since all processing is local and under your control, you exercise these rights directly through your own systems.

## 9. Privacy Rights (US & Global)

### 9.1 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
California residents should note that QuickBooks Time-Warp:
- Does not sell personal information
- Does not collect personal information
- Processes data locally under your control

### 9.2 Other Jurisdictions
Users in other jurisdictions with privacy laws (Canada, Australia, etc.) should note that:
- All processing is local and under your direct control
- No data is transmitted outside your organization
- You maintain full sovereignty over your data
